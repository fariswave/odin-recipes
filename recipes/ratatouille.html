Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a classic French dish, featuring a medley of tomatoes, zucchini, eggplant, bell peppers, and onions. Thinly sliced and seasoned with herbs, it offers a vibrant mix of flavors and textures. Often served as a side or main dish, ratatouille highlights the simplicity and natural goodness of seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 red onions
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 aubergines
  • 3 courgettes
  • 3 red or yellow peppers
  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • ½ a bunch of fresh basil , (15g)
  • olive oil
  • a few sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1 x 400 g tin of quality plum tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • ½ a lemon
