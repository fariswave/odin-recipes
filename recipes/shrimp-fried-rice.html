Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Shrimp Fried Rice is a flavorful Asian dish featuring succulent shrimp, stir-fried with aromatic jasmine rice, crisp vegetables, and a savory blend of soy sauce and sesame oil. This classic recipe creates a harmonious balance of textures and tastes, making it a popular and satisfying choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • ½ cup frozen corn
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced, or more to taste
  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions
  • 3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
